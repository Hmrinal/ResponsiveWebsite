The Vanguardian Kingdom

One web page for every book ever published. It's a lofty but achievable goal.

Vanguardian is an open project: the software is open, the data are open, the documentation is open, and we welcome your contribution. Whether you fix a typo, add a book, or write a widget--it's all welcome. With that you can read latest novels, research works and get an insight of various researches taking place. But we can't do it alone!

Pen it down

To build Open Library, we need hundreds of millions of book records, a wiki interface, and lots of people who are willing to contribute their time and effort to building the site.

Be an explorer

VG provides you free access to many journals, recent researches and novels. There are sections encompassed with Special Collections compiled by several individual journals and organizations.
